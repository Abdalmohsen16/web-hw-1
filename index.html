Brawlhalla

Brawlhalla is a free 2D platform fighting game that supports up to 8 local or online players with full cross-play for PC, Xbox One, Xbox Series X, PS5, PS4, iOS, Nintendo Switch, and Android. Climb the ranked ladder from Tin up to Platinum and beyond.

The best characters in the game (in my opinion) :

  • Artemis
  • Nix
  • Brynn
  • Diana
